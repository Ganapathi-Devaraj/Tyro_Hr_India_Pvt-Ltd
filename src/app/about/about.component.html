About Us

Tyro HR India Pvt Ltd, established in January 2022, is a leading organization offering a diverse range of HR services, including awards and certifications for outstanding HR professionals, payroll management, staffing solutions, and corporate training. We specialize in recognizing HR excellence and building a strong HR community through events, workshops, and knowledge-sharing platforms. In addition to HR expertise, we deliver customized software development solutions that align with specific business goals and industry requirements.

Our services cover web and mobile applications, full stack development, cloud integration, and enterprise solutions. Tyro HR is also an ISO certified organization, ensuring the highest standards in all our operations. We focus on talent development through comprehensive software development and corporate training programs. These training programs are designed to equip individuals with in-demand skills, real-time project experience, and career guidance. With a focus on quality, innovation, and impact, we empower both professionals and organizations to thrive in the digital era.

Our Vision

To revolutionize the HR and recruitment landscape by becoming the most trusted bridge between exceptional talent and leading organizations. Our vision is to cultivate a thriving HR community where innovation, integrity, and continuous growth empower businesses and individuals to achieve their full potential.

Our Mission

1. To connect exceptional talent with top-tier companies, fostering long-lasting partnerships that drive organizational success and career growth.

2. To deliver innovative, efficient, and customized HR solutions that align with the unique needs of clients and candidates.

3. To organize impactful HR events, leadership programs, and recognition awards, strengthening the HR community.

4. To uphold integrity, professionalism, and continuous learning while adapting to the evolving business environment.

5. To empower professionals and organizations nationwide, shaping a future where human potential is maximized.
